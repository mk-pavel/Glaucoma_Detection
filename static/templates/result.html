Understanding Your Results

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, essential for good vision. It's often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.

Early Detection

Early detection and treatment can help prevent vision loss. Regular eye exams that include measurements of your eye pressure are crucial.

Next Steps

If signs of glaucoma are detected, schedule an appointment with an ophthalmologist immediately. Early intervention is key.

Prevention

Regular comprehensive eye exams, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and protecting your eyes from injury can help reduce risk.
